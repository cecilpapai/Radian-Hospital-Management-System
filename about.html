We Offer High Quality Services

At Radian Hospital, we’re committed to redefining healthcare in Kenya by blending cutting-edge medical technology with patient-centered compassion. Since our founding in 2010, we’ve grown into a trusted institution serving 1200+ patients annually across Nairobi, Mombasa, Kisumu.

To Accomodate All Patients

"To deliver exceptional, accessible healthcare through cutting-edge technology, compassionate expertise, and community-centered innovation—ensuring every patient receives personalized care that transforms lives."

Help Our Customers Needs

At Radian Hospital, we are driven by a clear vision for Kenya's healthcare future. We pledge to combine medical excellence with compassionate service by continuously upgrading our technology and staff training to deliver world-class treatment outcomes. Our commitment extends beyond our walls through free community health initiatives and innovative mobile care units designed to reach underserved populations. We're determined to make quality healthcare accessible to all Kenyans by strengthening NHIF partnerships and developing affordable payment plans. As we grow to serve more regions across the country, we maintain an unwavering focus on sustainable practices that protect both patient health and our environment. Every decision we make and every goal we set is guided by our core belief: that exceptional healthcare is every Kenyan's right, not a privilege.
